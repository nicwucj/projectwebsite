About Us

Welcome to Sweet Cake Bake Shop, a beloved traditional cake shop in Singapore since 1980. We take pride in creating handcrafted cakes using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Each cake is made with the finest ingredients, ensuring a delightful taste that brings back fond memories of home and heritage. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, we are here to bring joy with every bite. Thank you for making us a part of your celebrations for over four decades!
